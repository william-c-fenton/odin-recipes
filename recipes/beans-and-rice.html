Beans and Rice

Description:

Classic Cuban-style beans and rice. This recipe is easy, cheap, and delicious to make. There is a reason why this is a staple food all over the world.

I will be using an instant pot for this recipe, but you can use a pressure cooker or regular pot to prepare the beans. Just note that you will have to adjust cooking times and techniques accordingly.

Ingredients:

  • 16 oz bag of black beans (other beans are fine too)
  • 1 cup dry rice (can really be any amount you're going to eat)
  • 3-5 slices bacon, a ham hock, 1.5 lb chicken breast... Some protein if you want it.
  • 1 onion, chunked
  • 3-5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes with chiles (optional)
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp oregano
  • 2 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 oz orange juice
  • 1 oz lemon or lime juice
  • 8 cups chicken stock or water (I use better than bullion)
  • 1 fresh tomato, diced (for garnish, optional)
  • 1 avocado, diced (for garnish, optional)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish, optional)
  • Shredded cheese (for garnish, optional)

Instructions:

  1. (OPTIONAL): Soak your beans overnight in some lightly-salted water. You can add baking soda as well to reduce potential gassiness.
    • You can also quick-soak in an instant pot by putting beans in with just enough water to cover and pressure cooking on high for about 5 minutes.
  2. Saute your onions and garlic until they are soft and fragrant.
  3. Add beans, stock, orange juice, lime juice, and spices. Add the can of tomato here if you have it.
  4. Pressure cook for 60 minutes on high pressure. Make sure the beans are tender, because they will take much longer than anything else to cook.
  5. Prepare your rice while the beans cook. You can do this on the stovetop or use a rice cooker.
  6. Once everything is finished cooking, spoon out a serving of rice and put the beans on top. Add garnishes now (they really take this to the next level).
  7. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
